About Me

I am a Computer Science Major at Miami University. While studying at Miami University, I have acquired the knowledge of the fundamentals of Computer Science, the ability to develop efficient software, and contribute to team based projects using Agile development. I have enjoyed studying many of the cutting-edge topics in Computer Science that interest me including Machine Learning, Cryptography, Networking, Operating Systems, and Web Applications, along with the skills to implement these concepts in a real world project.

As a result of my course work at Miami, I am proficient in multiple programming and scripting languages including Java, C++, Python, SQL, R, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and SAS, which I have used in both academic and personal projects. I have used these languages alongside development tools such as Eclipse, VS Code, Visual Studio, Jupyter Notebooks, and Git on both Windows and Linux operating systems.

I have also gained industry knowledge of the software project life cycle that includes the Agile development cycle, Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery, Version Control, Unit Tests, UML, and how to work as a part of a team to complete a complex software project. This knowledge was supplemented by completing a team based project that resulted in the creation of a functional application developed with a team of fellow students. During my summer break, I also utilized these concepts to develop an application that was utilized by my friends to streamline our fantasy football draft by making it virtual.

Throughout my academic and personal experiences, I have developed the ability to solve complex problems with an innovative solution that utilizes the strengths of others on my team to deliver a result that both the developer and the customer can be proud of. As a result of my studies and experiences so far, I am eager to help develop and improve real world systems, and continue learning new skills along the way.
